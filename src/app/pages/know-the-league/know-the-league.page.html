Know The League

Each team comprises of 12 players. The captain/ mentor of the team is chosen directly by the team owner. The uniqueness of the league lies in the fact that 7 players per team are amateurs that are selected through online qualifiers and other contests.

A nation wide leaderboard will be maintained and the players who earn the maximum points make it to the "draft".

Post Qualifiers, there will be a selection ceremony, where the team owners & mentors will choose the players from the various drafts.

Once the teams are selected and finalized, the players undergo training for the next 3 weeks with the mentors/coaches to prepare them for the finale. All selected players get a base salary as well as performance incentive (if the teams finish in the top 4 positions in the finale) Selection Ceremony will be in Delhi on March 1st, 2020.

The Poker Sports League grand finale will be in Kolkata in from March 15-23rd, 2020.

The total prize-pool will be Rs 4+ crores, with the top 4 teams being paid based on their finishing position.

All team members also get a percentage of the winning prize (predetermined split).
